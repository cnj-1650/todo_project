FocusFlow: To-Do + Timer + Flappy Bird
FocusFlow is a unique productivity tool designed to help you balance work and play. Manage your tasks with a clean To-Do list, stay on track with a built-in Pomodoro-style timer, and reward your hard work with a quick game of Flappy Bird during your breaks.
